Unit 1
Participating in this discussion deepened my understanding of the critical role data cleaning and wrangling play in effective data analytics. I previously viewed data preparation as a routine technical step, but I now recognize it as a foundational process that directly impacts the accuracy and reliability of analytical insights. Real‑world datasets are rarely perfect, and issues such as missing values, duplicates, outliers, and noisy data are inevitable, particularly in IoT‑driven environments. This discussion helped me appreciate that data cleaning is not a one‑time task but an ongoing process that requires continuous monitoring as new data is generated. I also became more aware of the risks associated with handling sensitive data, including security breaches and data corruption. Overall, this reflection has reshaped my perspective on data wrangling as a strategic and essential component of trustworthy, data‑driven decision‑making, reinforcing the idea that high‑quality data is fundamental to meaningful insight and organizational success (Infragistics, 2013).
